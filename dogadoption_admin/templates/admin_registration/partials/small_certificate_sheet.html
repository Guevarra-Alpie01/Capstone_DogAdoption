Owner Responsibilities
Under Republic Act 9482 (Anti-Rabies Act of 2007), pet owners must:
  1. Submit dogs for regular or annual rabies vaccination.
  2. Register and license their dogs.
  3. Do not allow dogs to roam in public places without a leash.
  4. Provide proper care, food, and shelter.
  5. Report biting incidents and submit dogs for observation when required.
