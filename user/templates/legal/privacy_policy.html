This page explains what information Bayawan Vet collects, how it is used, and how users can request access or deletion of their account data.

Information we collect

When you create an account or use the site, we may collect:

  • Account details such as username, name, and email address
  • Profile information you choose to add
  • Posts, requests, comments, and other content you submit
  • Technical information needed to keep the site secure and functional

How we use information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To display your posts and requests on the site
  • To communicate account-related updates
  • To help maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug problems

Sharing and retention

We do not sell your personal information. Some information you post publicly on the site may remain visible to other users until it is removed or deleted. We keep data only as long as needed to operate the service, meet legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

Your choices

  • You can request access or deletion of your account data using the contact below.
  • If you delete your account, some records may remain where required by law or for security.
