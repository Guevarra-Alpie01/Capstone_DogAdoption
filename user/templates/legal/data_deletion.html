Data Deletion Instructions

If you want Bayawan Vet to delete your account data, use the steps below. This page is provided for Meta review and for users who want to remove their information from the service.

How to request deletion

1. Send an email request

Email alpieguevarra.dev@gmail.com and include the subject line Account Deletion Request.

2. Include the account details

Include the email address tied to your Bayawan Vet account so we can verify the request and find the correct record.

3. We review and delete

After verification, we will delete the account data we control and confirm once the request has been completed.

What may be removed

  • Your account profile data
  • Content you submitted through the site
  • Associated records that can be deleted from our system

What may remain

Some records may remain where we must keep them for legal, security, or operational reasons. Public content that has already been shared externally may also persist outside our control.

Need help?

If you cannot access the email linked to your account, contact us from another email and include enough information for us to verify ownership.
